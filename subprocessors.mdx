# Subprocessors

Kuren uses the following third-party service providers to deliver our services. These subprocessors may have access to your data as necessary to perform their functions.

## LLM Providers

Kuren integrates with the following AI model providers to power our intelligent features:

- **Google** - Gemini models and Google AI services
- **OpenAI** - GPT and other OpenAI models
- **Anthropic** - Claude models
- **AWS** - Amazon Bedrock and AWS AI services
- **XAi** - Grok and XAi models
- **Groq** - High-performance LLM inference
- **Cerebras** - AI acceleration and model inference
